client-2.in is a Telugu online e-paper dedicated to delivering fast, reliable news to readers in their own language.

It focuses on local news from towns and villages across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, ensuring regional issues and voices are highlighted.

At the same time, it covers major national developments from across India, helping readers stay informed about politics, economy, technology, and society.

International news is also included so Telugu readers can easily follow important global events without language barriers.

The platform aims to provide clear, unbiased coverage with a special emphasis on accuracy and credibility.

Designed as a digital e-paper, it offers a newspaper-like reading experience that works smoothly on mobiles, tablets, and desktops.

The vision of client-2.in is to make quality journalism accessible to every Telugu speaker, wherever they live.
